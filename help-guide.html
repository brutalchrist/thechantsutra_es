1 Daily Guidance

From the home page, click on the 'Guidance' Icon

2 Goals

2.1 Adding New Goal

2.1.1From the home page, click on the Goals icon.

2.1.2 Click on (+) icon

2.1.3 Add Screen will be displayed. Enter the details and click on OK from the title bar

2.2 Update existing goal

2.2.1 Click on the goals icon from the homepage -> goals screen will display existing goals.

2.2.2 Select the goal (checkbox) and then click on the edit icon displayed at the bottom

2.2.3 Edit the details and click on OK

2.3 Marking Goal as Achieved/Sharing on Facebook/Tweet Goal/Reset/ Delete / Email Goal

2.3.1 Click on the goals icon from the homepage -> goals screen will display existing goals.

2.3.2 Select the goal (checkbox) and then click on the appropriate icon from the options displayed at the bottom of the screen

2.4 Selecting Goals for Daimoku

There are two ways to achieve this, as explained below

2.4.1 Goals Screen

2.4.1.1 From the home page, click on the Goals icon, goal screen will be displayed with existing goal

2.4.1.2 Select the goal you would like to chant (Daimoku) for and then click on the chant option displayed at the bottom of screen

2.4.1.3 You will be directed to chant screen, click on the start button. Once done chanting, click on the stop button. The chant duration will be added to the goal chant duration.

2.4.2 Chant Screen

2.4.2.1 From the home page, clock on Chant Icon. Chant Screen will be displayed

2.4.2.2 Click on the goals tab (don't click on start button)

2.4.2.3 Select the goals you want to chant for

2.4.2.4 Click on the StopWatch tab again and click on start button to start chanting

3 Chant Screen

Click on the start button to start chanting.

Click on stop button to stop chanting.

If target time is selected, the phone will vibrate when the chant time matches the target time selected.

Click on the Set Audio – to chant with pre-recorded Gongyo and Daimoku.

4 Meetings

4.1 Add a new meeting –

4.1.1 Click on Meeting Icon on the home page and please press (+) on the top menu.

4.1.2 Agenda for the meeting, Location, Date, Time can be set as per the schedule.

4.1.3 Invite other friend(s) using the icon

Select participants and click on OK.

Click Save to create the meeting:

4.2 Edit Meeting

From the home page, click on the meetings icon.

Tap on the meeting you would like to update. It would open the update screen. Make changes and click save

4.3 Send SMS to meeting Participants

From the home page, click on the meetings icon.

Tap on the meeting you would like to update. It would open the update screen. Click on SMS icon

4.4 Record Attendance

From the home page, click on the meetings icon.

Tap on the meeting to record the attendance for. It would open the update screen. Click on the checkboxes next to invitee's name in 'Participant List\Record Attendance' section to mark the attendance.

4.5 Email Attendance

From the home page, click on the meetings icon. Select the meeting (checkbox) and click on the email icon

5 Center(s)

Please select the country you want to choose – it will open it up in google map. You may use to navigate to the desired center.

6 Settings

Under settings you have to put your user id and password to access to post on Facebook and Twitter from this app.

Press check box to login to Facebook /Twitter.

Post Status – when I start chanting: Posts on your Facebook \Twitter whenever you start chanting.

Post Status – when I achieve the goal : Posts your victory on the Facebook\Twitter.
