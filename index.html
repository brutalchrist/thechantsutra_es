The ChantSutra

Chanting application

iPhone application is in progress and it will be available to members soon. Please check back later. Thanks for your support.

Features

Daily guidance from SGI
Set up goals for chanting
  • Make personal or community goals
  • Track/manage goals by specifying start / end date
  • When achieved - share it with friends via Facebook or Twitter
Chant
  • Stop watch to track time for chanting
  • Set Audio - pick the prayers ( chanting/ Gongyo/Personal recorded prayers)
  • Report
    • Graphical display of time of chanting per day
    • Pick any goal and track time.
Prayer
  • Player can play the existing/loaded prayers ( slow\fast Gongyo\chanting)
  • Record your own chanting or prayers.
  • Play/Stop/ Repeat - any prayer
Meetings
  • Setup meetings for your district\chapter.
  • Invite your members to this meeting
  • Record the attendance
  • Email the report of attendance to your leader or any email
Video
  • MyChantSutra channel to give introduction to Buddhism and SGI

FAQ's

Can I ask for more features?

Yes, please send us a request at support@thechantsutra.com or using settings in the app to request a feature.

Do I have to pay for any feature?

No, this app is our dedication for SGI members.

Can I record my own district or members chanting?

Yes, please use the record feature in the prayers. The prayer will be available to be selected in chanting screen.

Can I contribute to this app?

Yes, we are looking for members to help in testing and reporting bugs.

Donate

As our The ChantSutra app is free for all, there is a cost towards the maintenance and future releases. If you want to contribute towards this wonderful cause, please donate. Thank you!!

Contact

If you have to report any bug or request any new feature - you can reach us at support@thechantsutra.com. We try to revert back within 24 hours.
